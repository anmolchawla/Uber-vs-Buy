About Line Chart

Ten countries were chosen from The World Bank - World Development Indicators.Multi Series Line chart shows different countries with thier % rural population along the time axis at an interval of five years from 1992 to 2017.A relevant color scheme was also implemented, accompanied with a legend. Line chart: As you hover over each circle, a tooltip will display relevant infromation on the circle. When you click on the line it will temporarily become thicker giving it a pop effect and making it stand out amongst the rest. As shown Portugal and China have had sharp decrease in their % rural population over time.
